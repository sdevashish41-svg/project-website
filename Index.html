Warning Signs

Symptoms That Need a Cardiologist

If you experience any of these symptoms, consult Dr. Kamlesh Singh immediately for expert cardiac evaluation and care.

Chest Pain or Discomfort

Persistent or intermittent chest pain, pressure, or discomfort can be a sign of serious heart issues requiring immediate attention.

Shortness of Breath

Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath, especially with minimal exertion, could indicate heart or lung problems.

Irregular Heartbeat

Palpitations, fluttering, or a sensation of your heart racing or beating irregularly may suggest arrhythmia.

Extreme Fatigue

Unexplained fatigue or weakness, particularly if severe or accompanied by other symptoms, may warrant cardiac evaluation.

Dizziness or Fainting

Sudden dizziness, lightheadedness, or fainting spells can be related to heart issues and should be evaluated promptly.

Swelling (Edema)

Swelling in the legs, ankles, feet, or abdomen could be a sign of heart failure and requires immediate medical attention.
